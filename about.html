Life is all about choices, Jerry and Jhanet in June 29, 2004, encouraged by a dear German friend, took opportunity to venture in automotive interior business. Full of energy and in high spirit the couple opened their first facility in Dasmariñas, Cavite. Together, forever, they loudly declare "My choice, my Lederland" Lederland with a big dream, started small. So soon Lederland was able to point out that leather is in and that people should live in style while enjoying the feel and comfort of a genuine leather.

Like wild fire, the talks about lederland spread all over cavite and the nearby Metro Manila.Custom built. Best in class and best value became the by-word for Lederland. My choice, my Lederland, became "Our choice, our Lederland."

Thrilled with accelerated growth, Lederland moved to West Ave. Quezon City and then later opened new facilities in Banawe, Mindanao Ave., and Don Antonio, Quezon City. The superior craftsmanship and elegant style of Lederland created a bandwagon of high-end loyal customers with luxurious lifestyles.

Role model management styles, customer caring, focus for employees' growth and a burning passion to deliver high quality products and excellent services are the trademarks of Lederland.
