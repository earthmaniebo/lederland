LEDERLAND DON ANTONIO NOW OPEN!

On September 27, 2014, Saturday, Lederland received another huge blessing as we celebrated the grand opening of another branch opened at Don Antonio Sports Center, Q.C. Everyone was thrilled and excited for the celebration of the said event.


Lederland started the day with a mass led by Father July to give thanks for another opportunity to serve people in search of comfort, enjoyment and luxury in terms of automotive interior. Also, to give back the praises and glory to our one and only creator Jesus Christ.




After the mass, The Ribbon Cutting was held to inaugurate the opening to the general public of the new branch. Father July, then, started blessing the whole establishment.


Finally, It was time for the part everybody was waiting for. The Banquet.
